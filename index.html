Jennifer's Body (2009)

Comedy Horror

A newly-possessed high-school cheerleader turns into a succubus who specializes in killing her male classmates. Can her best friend put an end to the horror?

Director: Karyn Kusama.
Writer: Diablo Cody.
Stars: Megan Fox, Amanda Seyfried, Adam Brody.

  • Despite mixed critical reception, the film has gained a strong cult following over the years. It even garnered an unofficial musical in 2018.

  • To prepare for her role as a possessed living-dead teenager, Megan Fox lost around 15 lbs, bringing her weight down to a near-frail 97 lbs, and stayed out of the sun for four months to keep her skin pale.

  • Diablo Cody wrote the screenplay for the film in 2006, the same year she wrote Juno (2007).

  • Emma Stone was considered for the role of Anita "Needy" Lesnicki.

  • The director's cut of the film, which added five extra minutes of footage, was removed from the home video release and from Australian stores and supermarkets due to the graphic sex scene and the violence.

  • There was no makeout scene between Jennifer and Needy in Diablo Cody's initial draft. As she was re-writing the script, she cranked the sexual tension up to eleven and included the aforementioned sex scene, before settling with the two of them kissing instead.
